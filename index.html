Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to add custom overused word/words to the list?

    1. Go to 'WORDS' section.
    2. In input area, type word/words of your choosing, using comma or whitespace as separator.
    3. Click on 'ADD WORDS' button.
    4. Custom word/words can be found now on overused words list.

  • How to highlight overused or repeated words?

    In the list of repeated or overused words, click on the word. The selection will be visible on the list and in the text area.

  • How to delete overused words?

    Each custom or pre defined overused word have a - icon on right side. Click on it, to delete a word.

  • How to add pre defined overused words?

    In the "WORD" section, under the area for entering custom overused words you can find pre defined words.
