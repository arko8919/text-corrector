    Adding overused words to the list

    1. Go to overused words section.
    2. In input area, type word/words of your choosing (u can use a comma or whitespace as separator).
    3. Click on add button.
    4. Word/Words can be found on overused words list.

    Repeated words - to find matches in text, click word on the repeated words list.

    Overused words - to find matches in the text, check the box on the list of words that are being overused.
